<p><p>The fiberglass rigid board insulation market mainly comprises semi-rigid boards and rigid boards. Semi-rigid boards are flexible yet provide sufficient support, making them ideal for applications where some contour fitting is needed, such as around ducts. Rigid boards, on the other hand, are sturdy and offer high thermal resistance, suitable for a range of applications including walls, roofs, and foundations. Both types are valued for their energy efficiency, durability, and resistance to moisture, contributing to improved building performance.</p></p>
