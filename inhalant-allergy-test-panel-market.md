<p><ul><li>Total IgE</li><li>Specific IgE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inhalant Allergy Test Panel market comprises two main types: Total IgE tests and Specific IgE tests. Total IgE tests measure the overall level of immunoglobulin E in the blood, which can indicate a general predisposition to allergies. Specific IgE tests, on the other hand, focus on the immune response to particular allergens, such as pollen, dust mites, or pet dander. Together, these tests help diagnose allergic conditions and guide treatment strategies for individuals with respiratory allergies.</p></p>
