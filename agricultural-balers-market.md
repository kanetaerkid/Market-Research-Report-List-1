<p><p>The agricultural balers market is primarily divided into two types: round balers and square balers. Round balers create cylindrical bales, which are easier to handle and transport, making them ideal for livestock feed and forage applications. Square balers produce rectangular bales, offering higher density and uniformity, suitable for storage and transportation. Each type caters to different operational needs and preferences, influencing their adoption among farmers based on factors such as crop type, storage conditions, and equipment compatibility.</p></p>
