<p><ul><li>BOPP Based</li><li>BOPET Based</li><li>BOPA Based</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot laminated film market is segmented into several types based on the substrate. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) based films are widely used for packaging due to their excellent clarity and moisture barrier properties. BOPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyester) based films offer enhanced strength and chemical resistance, ideal for high-tech applications. BOPA (Biaxially Oriented Polyamide) based films provide superior flexibility and puncture resistance, commonly used in food packaging. Other types may include specialty films designed for niche applications, each serving distinct packaging needs.</p></p>
