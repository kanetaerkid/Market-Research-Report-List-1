<p><strong>The Polyurethane Resin Composite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pultrusion</li><li>Winding</li><li>Perfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane resin composite market features several processing techniques, including pultrusion, winding, and perfusion. </p><p>Pultrusion involves continuous molding, producing consistent profiles with high strength and rigidity. Winding is used to create cylindrical structures by layering resin-infused fibers, ideal for applications like tanks and pipes. Perfusion, or infusion, combines resin and fiber in a vacuum environment, ensuring thorough wetting and reducing voids, resulting in lightweight, high-performance composites suitable for automotive and aerospace industries. Each method caters to specific applications and requirements in the composite market.</p></p>
