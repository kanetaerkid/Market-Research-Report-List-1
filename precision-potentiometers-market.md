<p><p>The precision potentiometers market is divided into multi-turn and single-turn types. Multi-turn precision potentiometers allow for fine adjustments over several rotations, making them ideal for applications requiring high precision, such as in industrial and aerospace instruments. In contrast, single-turn precision potentiometers offer a single full rotation, suitable for simpler applications where less precision is needed, like volume controls in audio equipment. Both types are essential in various sectors, including electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, for precise measurement and control.</p></p>
