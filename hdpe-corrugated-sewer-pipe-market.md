<p><p>The HDPE corrugated sewer pipe market comprises two main types: single wall and double wall corrugated pipes. Single wall corrugated pipes are characterized by their lightweight structure and are typically used for drainage and sewage applications. In contrast, double wall corrugated pipes feature an inner smooth wall and an outer corrugated structure, offering enhanced strength and resistance to external loads. Both types are valued for their durability, flexibility, and resistance to corrosion, making them suitable for various underground drainage systems.</p></p>
