<p><strong>The Automatic CPR Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatically Driven</li><li>Electrically Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic CPR devices are categorized into two main types: pneumatically driven and electrically powered. Pneumatically driven devices utilize compressed air to deliver compressions, offering a lightweight and portable design. They effectively adapt to the patient's anatomy for optimal performance. Electrically powered devices, on the other hand, use electric motors to provide consistent and controlled chest compressions, ensuring adherence to guidelines and improved efficiency. Both types enhance the quality of CPR delivered in critical situations, optimizing patient outcomes during cardiac arrest.</p></p>
