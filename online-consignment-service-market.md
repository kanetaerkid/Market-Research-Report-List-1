<p><strong>The Online Consignment Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outward Consignment</li><li>Inward Consignment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Online consignment services operate through two main market types: outward consignment and inward consignment. In outward consignment, sellers send their products to a platform where they are sold on their behalf, receiving payment only upon successful sales. Conversely, inward consignment involves a service where platforms acquire goods, sell them, and subsequently return unsold items to the original sellers. Both models facilitate inventory management and extend market reach for sellers while offering consumers a diverse product selection online.</p></p>
