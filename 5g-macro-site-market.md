<p><p>In the 5G macro site market, there are two primary types: Stand Alone (SA) and Non-Stand Alone (NSA). SA architecture operates independently, utilizing a 5G core network for enhanced performance and low latency, providing full 5G capabilities. In contrast, NSA leverages existing 4G infrastructure alongside 5G technology, allowing for faster deployment but with some limitations in speed and capacity. Both market types cater to different deployment strategies and customer needs, influencing the evolution of telecommunications networks.</p></p>
