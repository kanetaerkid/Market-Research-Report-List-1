<p><p>The beverage kegs market is primarily divided into two types: plastic kegs and metal kegs. Plastic kegs are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and easy to transport, making them ideal for breweries looking to reduce shipping costs. They are often used for one-way distribution and offer better insulation, preserving the beverage's quality. Metal kegs, typically made of stainless steel, are durable and reusable, commonly employed in traditional settings. They provide superior protection, are suited for long-term storage, and maintain carbonation effectively.</p></p>
