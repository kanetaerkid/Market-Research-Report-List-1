<p><strong>The Grouting Additive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-Component</li><li>Two-Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The grouting additive market comprises two primary types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component additives are pre-mixed solutions that require no additional components for application, simplifying the mixing and application process. In contrast, two-component additives involve a catalyst or hardener that must be combined with a base material before use, allowing for enhanced customization and performance characteristics. Each type serves specific applications depending on project requirements, setting times, and desired strength, influencing the choice for contractors and builders.</p></p>
