<p><p>The electrolysis of water market encompasses two main types: Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers and Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolyzers. Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers utilize a liquid alkaline electrolyte, offering cost-effectiveness and long-term stability, making them suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, PEM Electrolyzers leverage a solid electrolyte for higher efficiency and quicker response times, ideal for applications requiring high purity hydrogen and flexibility. Both technologies contribute to clean hydrogen production, driving advancements in renewable energy utilization.</p></p>
