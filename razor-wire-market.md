<p><p>The Razor Wire market is primarily categorized into two types: Concertina Razor Wire and Flat Razor Wire. Concertina Razor Wire features a coiled design that forms a series of spirals, ideal for perimeter security due to its deterrent effect and ease of installation. In contrast, Flat Razor Wire consists of flat strips with sharp barbs, providing a more discreet security option typically used in high-security applications. Both types serve to enhance security through their formidable physical barriers while addressing varying installation requirements.</p></p>
