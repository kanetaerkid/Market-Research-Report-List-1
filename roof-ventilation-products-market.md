<p><p>The roof ventilation products market encompasses three primary types: roof vents, roof fans, and other ventilation solutions. Roof vents are designed to allow the escape of hot, stale air from attics, facilitating temperature regulation. Roof fans actively push air out, enhancing airflow and reducing moisture buildup, which is crucial for preventing mold. Other products may include solar-powered vents or ridge vents, providing additional options for efficient roof ventilation. Together, these products improve energy efficiency and prolong the lifespan of roofing materials.</p></p>
