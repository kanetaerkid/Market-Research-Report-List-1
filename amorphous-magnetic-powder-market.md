<p><strong>The Amorphous Magnetic Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soft Magnetic Powders</li><li>Hard Magnetic Powders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The amorphous magnetic powder market is segmented into two main types: soft magnetic powders and hard magnetic powders. Soft magnetic powders are characterized by their high magnetic permeability and low coercivity, making them suitable for applications that require efficient magnetization and demagnetization, such as transformers and inductors. Hard magnetic powders, on the other hand, possess high coercivity and retain magnetization, ideal for permanent magnets used in motors, sensors, and storage devices. Both types are essential for diverse technological applications.</p></p>
