<p><p>The micro gripper market comprises two primary types: pneumatic and electric micro grippers. Pneumatic micro grippers utilize compressed air to create movement and gripping force, offering quick actuation and high gripping precision, ideal for applications in automation and robotics. In contrast, electric micro grippers are powered by motors or actuators, providing precise control over positioning and speed, making them suitable for delicate operations in industries like electronics assembly and medical device manufacturing, where accuracy is critical.</p></p>
