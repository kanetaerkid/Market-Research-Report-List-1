<p><strong>The Commercial Aluminum Entrance Mat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded</li><li>Non-Embedded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Aluminum Entrance Mat market is divided into two main types: embedded and non-embedded mats. Embedded mats are designed to be installed flush with the surrounding flooring, providing a seamless appearance and minimizing tripping hazards. They are commonly used in high-traffic areas. Non-embedded mats, on the other hand, sit on top of the floor surface and can be easily relocated for cleaning or maintenance. Both types serve to enhance indoor cleanliness and safety by trapping dirt and moisture at entry points.</p></p>
