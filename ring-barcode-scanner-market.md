<p><p>The ring barcode scanner market is categorized into two main types: 1D and 2D ring barcode scanners. 1D ring barcode scanners are designed to read traditional linear barcodes, which consist of vertical lines and spaces, typically found on retail products. In contrast, 2D ring barcode scanners can decode advanced matrix codes, such as QR codes, allowing for greater data storage and versatility. Both types enhance operational efficiency in industries like retail, logistics, and warehousing by enabling hands-free scanning.</p></p>
