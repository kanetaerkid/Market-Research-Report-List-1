<p><p>The air-conditioning and refrigeration market comprises various system types catering to diverse cooling needs. Airside systems manage air distribution, while chillers provide centralized cooling for larger spaces. Portable units offer flexibility for temporary cooling, while single packaged systems house all components in a single unit, suitable for small areas. Split systems separate the indoor and outdoor components, providing efficient cooling for residential and commercial spaces. Window units are compact installations designed for individual rooms, delivering effective localized cooling.</p></p>
