<p><p>MS Polymer, known for its superior properties, is categorized into two primary market types: extrusion grade and injection molding grade. Extrusion grade MS Polymer is primarily used for continuous processes, ideal for producing sheets, profiles, and seals due to its excellent flow characteristics. In contrast, injection molding grade is designed for shaping complex parts, offering precise detail and strength in molds. Both grades effectively harness the versatile capabilities of MS Polymer in diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>
