<p><p>The commercial greenhouse market is primarily divided into two main types: glass greenhouses and plastic greenhouses. Glass greenhouses offer durability and excellent light transmission, making them suitable for high-value crops and regions with variable weather conditions. However, they have higher initial costs. Plastic greenhouses, usually made from polycarbonate or polyethylene, are more affordable and lightweight, providing good thermal regulation and ease of installation. They are ideal for various crops and can be tailored to specific climate needs, enhancing versatility in agricultural practices.</p></p>
