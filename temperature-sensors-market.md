<p><p>The temperature sensors market includes various types: Negative Temperature Coefficient (NTC) thermistors, which decrease resistance with rising temperature; Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs), utilizing metal resistance changes for accurate measurements; thermocouples, which generate voltage based on temperature differences between two junctions; and semiconductor-based sensors, leveraging semiconductor properties for precise temperature reading. Each type serves specific applications, from industrial processes to consumer electronics, influencing market dynamics based on accuracy, range, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
