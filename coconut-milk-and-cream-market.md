<p><p>Coconut milk and cream are popular plant-based dairy alternatives derived from the flesh of coconuts. Coconut milk is a liquid made by blending coconut flesh with water, while coconut cream contains a higher fat content and is thicker, made by simmering coconut flesh in water and straining it. Both products are used in various culinary applications, including beverages, desserts, and savory dishes, appealing to health-conscious consumers due to their dairy-free, lactose-free, and vegan-friendly attributes.</p><p>The Coconut Milk and Cream Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by rising consumer demand for plant-based diets and the increasing popularity of vegan and lactose-free alternatives. Awareness around health benefits associated with coconut products, such as their favorable fatty acid profile and potential anti-inflammatory properties, further fuels this trend. Overall, the future of coconut milk and cream appears promising as consumer preferences evolve towards healthier options.</p></p>
