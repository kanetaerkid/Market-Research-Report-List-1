<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Non-absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suture button market is categorized into two primary types: absorbable and non-absorbable. Absorbable suture buttons are designed to break down over time in the body, eliminating the need for removal and suitable for temporary applications. In contrast, non-absorbable suture buttons remain permanently in the body, providing lasting support for long-term healing, typically used in more complex surgical procedures. The choice between these types depends on the surgical context, patient needs, and specific tissue recovery requirements.</p></p>
