<p><strong>The Non-destructive Testing for Oil and Gas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radiographic Inspection (RT)</li><li>Ultrasonic Testing (UT)</li><li>Magnetic Particle Testing (MT)</li><li>Liquid Penetrant Testing (PT)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-destructive testing (NDT) in the oil and gas market encompasses various techniques that assess material integrity without causing damage. Radiographic Inspection (RT) uses X-rays or gamma rays to reveal internal flaws. Ultrasonic Testing (UT) employs high-frequency sound waves to detect imperfections. Magnetic Particle Testing (MT) identifies surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials using magnetic fields and iron particles. Liquid Penetrant Testing (PT) highlights surface-breaking flaws by using dye or fluorescent agents. Other NDT methods include eddy current testing and visual inspection, enhancing safety and compliance.</p></p>
