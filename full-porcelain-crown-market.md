<p><p>The full porcelain crown market comprises several types, each known for unique properties. Silica-based crowns are lightweight and aesthetic, while alumina crowns offer increased strength and durability. Zirconia crowns are highly resilient and suitable for posterior teeth. Monolithic zirconia crowns combine strength with aesthetics, making them versatile for various dental applications. Lithium-disilicate crowns provide excellent aesthetics and are favored for anterior restorations due to their translucency. Together, these materials cater to diverse clinical needs, balancing aesthetics and performance.</p></p>
