<p><p>The Patient Management System (PMS) market is primarily divided into two types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises solutions. Cloud-Based PMS offer accessibility from anywhere with an internet connection, facilitating real-time data sharing and updates, often with lower upfront costs and easy scalability. In contrast, On-Premises systems are installed locally on hospital servers, providing greater control over data and security but requiring significant upfront investment and IT resources for maintenance. Each type serves distinct needs based on budget, security, and accessibility preferences.</p></p>
