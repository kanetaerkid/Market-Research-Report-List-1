<p><strong>The RTV Silicone Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RTV-1</li><li>RTV-2</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RTV silicone rubber market is divided into two main types: RTV-1 and RTV-2. RTV-1, or room temperature vulcanizing silicone, is a single-component system that cures upon exposure to moisture, making it ideal for applications like sealants and coatings. RTV-2 consists of a two-component system, requiring a mix of base and curing agent, offering higher strength and durability for molds and industrial applications. Both types are widely used in automotive, construction, and consumer goods due to their versatile properties.</p></p>
