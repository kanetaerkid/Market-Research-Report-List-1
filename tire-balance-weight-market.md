<p><ul><li>Clip-On Type</li><li>Adhesive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tire balance weight market consists primarily of two types: clip-on and adhesive weights. Clip-on weights are typically secured to the rim's outer flange using a metal clip, making them easy to install and adjust. Conversely, adhesive weights are mounted using a strong adhesive backing, allowing for a cleaner appearance and reduced aerodynamic drag. Both types serve the same purpose of ensuring even tire wear and improved vehicle handling by balancing the weight distribution of a tire and wheel assembly.</p></p>
