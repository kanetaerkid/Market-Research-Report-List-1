<p><p>The immunosuppressive drugs market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Calcineurin Inhibitors** (e.g., tacrolimus, cyclosporine) prevent T-cell activation, crucial for organ transplant success.  </p><p>2. **Antiproliferative Agents** (e.g., azathioprine, mycophenolate mofetil) inhibit the proliferation of immune cells to reduce rejection.  </p><p>3. **mTOR Inhibitors** (e.g., everolimus, sirolimus) target the mTOR pathway, impairing T-cell activation and proliferation.  </p><p>4. **Antibodies** (e.g., basiliximab, antithymocyte globulin) modulate immune response through targeted action.  </p><p>5. **Steroids** (e.g., prednisone) effectively reduce inflammation and immune response, commonly used in various conditions.</p></p>
