<p><p>The electron beam welding machine market is divided into two main types based on vacuum levels: High Vacuum Beam Welding Machines and Medium Vacuum Beam Welding Machines. High Vacuum Beam Welding Machines operate in a vacuum of 10^-5 to 10^-7 Torr, ensuring minimal contamination and enabling precise, deep penetration welds for complex materials like titanium and alloys. Medium Vacuum Beam Welding Machines operate in a vacuum range of 10^-3 to 10^-5 Torr, suitable for less critical applications, offering a balance between performance and cost.</p></p>
