<p><p>The food grade recycled polypropylene (PP) market comprises two primary types: post-industrial resin (PIR) and post-consumption resin (PCR). PIR originates from manufacturing excess and production scraps that are reclaimed and processed for reuse, ensuring high quality and consistency. PCR, on the other hand, is derived from consumer waste, such as used packaging, which undergoes recycling to produce new materials. Both types contribute to environmental sustainability while meeting regulatory standards for food safety in packaging applications.</p></p>
