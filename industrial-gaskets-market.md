<p><p>The industrial gaskets market is categorized into three types: metallic, non-metallic, and semi-metallic gaskets. Metallic gaskets, made from materials like stainless steel, offer excellent durability and resistance to extreme temperatures and pressures, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Non-metallic gaskets, typically composed of rubber or fiber materials, are used in less demanding environments due to their flexibility and sealing capabilities. Semi-metallic gaskets blend both materials, providing enhanced performance for specific applications, ensuring effective sealing under varying conditions.</p></p>
