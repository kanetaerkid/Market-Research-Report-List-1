<p><p>The digital dose inhalers market primarily includes two types: Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) and Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs). MDIs deliver a specific aerosolized medication dose, often featuring dose counters and connectivity for tracking usage. In contrast, DPIs release medication in a powdered form, requiring patients to inhale forcefully to receive the correct dosage. Both inhaler types benefit from digital enhancements, enabling better adherence monitoring, personalized medication plans, and improved patient engagement, ultimately optimizing respiratory disease management.</p></p>
